How It Works

When a guest connects to the captive portal, domains in the walled garden are resolved and added to nftables rules, allowing traffic to these IPs without authentication. The captive portal redirect applies only to unmatched traffic. Domains are automatically resolved to IP addresses when added and periodically synced.
